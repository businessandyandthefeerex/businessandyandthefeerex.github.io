Archie Bros is an arcade chain with bowling, mini putt and arcade games. Their menu is exactly what you'd expect - a variety of fried stuff, burgers and pizza, all a little bit overpriced. What you wouldn't expect is the vessels for their cocktails. Our drinks came in an elephant and a rollerskate, and we were living for it. What we weren't living for was the flavour - talk about sweet! One of them was literally topped with candyfloss, so perhaps the eye-blistering levels of sugar weren't unexpected. 

The food was so-so. We did appreciate the fried chicken, but the cauliflower, which was in the 'nude' style, was under-flavoured. The nacho chips were a large serve, but without a lot of substance. There was no mince (either vegetarian or meatatarian), instead just a bunch of salsa, cheese, bacon and lumps of avocado or sour cream. These were dotted about unevenly which was upsetting - either put it all in one place and we will dsitribute it ourselves, or dole it out diffusely. Don't stockpile it in a few key strongholds and leave us to work it out, we're deep into the elephant/rollerskate and can't deal with that maths.
