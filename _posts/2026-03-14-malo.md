Duck tostadas  
Pumpkin gnocchi  
Chips  

Mate, you’ve got to get this tostada in you. The pulled pork was juicy, the vegetables fresh, the sauce sweet, and the whole thing together was delicious. The gnocchi was less good, it was hard to pinpoint but there was something a bit weird about the flavour that the Fee-Rex in particular didn’t like. But then there were the chips. These were the best chips we’d had in some time. Obviously genuinely hand cut, they were deliciously chunky, salty and crispy in just the right amounts.
