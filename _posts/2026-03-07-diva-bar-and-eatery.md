Let’s dive straight in. How many olives should be on bruschetta? If you answered 1000 black and 1000000 green then you must work at Divas. What was that all about? Furthermore, the bread was doughy and not toasted with unwelcome raw big pieces of red onion. Booooo. 

While we’re complaining, those fish tacos were awful. The fish was sans flavour and sans moisture, just a dry bland mouthful encased in an over-dry tortilla. Mexico is owed an apology. China is also owed an apology as tomato sauce has no place in a bao bun. At least the chicken was good though. 

Let’s wrap it up with a compliment. The arancini was actually pretty good - flavourful inside, hot and crispy outside. We’d happily eat that again, but everything else can go straight in the bin.
