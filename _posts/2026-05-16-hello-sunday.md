Mushrooms on toast  
Beef burger with chips  

Hello Sunday is a very popular brunch spot, and we can see why. The menu looks fab, we’d love to go back and try more 

The standout was the mushrooms on toast which had this surprising little pesto swirl that really made the flavours sing. The burger was good, although the patty itself was a touch dry. Truffle mayo for the fries nom nom nom. 

The cocktail menu was outstanding, we had three and they were all delicious especially the salted caramel espresso martini which we practically drank in one, we couldn’t put that thing down 

Bookings recommended
